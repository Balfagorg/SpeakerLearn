EDU-EQ Speaker Calibration

How This Works

Enter how your speaker changes sound.

Example: If your speaker boosts 8kHz by +2 dB, move the slider to +2.

EDU-EQ will automatically generate the opposite correction EQ below.

Example:

Speaker response: +2 dB at 8kHz
Correction EQ: -2 dB at 8kHz

This makes audio sound more consistent across different speakers.
