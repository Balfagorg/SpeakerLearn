Our Mission

Music the way the
artist intended

Every speaker system is unique. From the driver diameter and cabinet resonance to the DSP firmware baked into your Bluetooth earbuds — each device imparts its own tonal signature onto every song you play.

FreqSync builds a precision frequency profile for your specific speaker hardware, then intelligently applies real-time correction so that a snare hit lands exactly as the recording engineer heard it in the studio.

Whether you're mixing on studio monitors, listening on a portable Bluetooth speaker, or comparing tracks across an entire sound system, FreqSync keeps every playback device honest.
